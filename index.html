Privacy Policy · 隐私政策

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Summary

NutriSnap is a personal food and nutrition tracker. We do not run any server and we do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Everything you log stays on your device. AI food recognition runs through your own third-party AI account (you provide the API key).

1. Who we are

NutriSnap ("the app", "we") is an independent mobile application for iOS and Android. Contact: regtechcrypto@gmail.com.

2. Data stored on your device

The app stores the following locally on your device only, and never transmits it to us:

  • Your food diary entries (food names, weights, calories, protein, fat, carbs, meal, date) and any photos you attach to an entry;
  • Your settings (daily goals, reminder times, language);
  • The AI provider you choose and its API key.

We have no access to this data. Removing an entry, or uninstalling the app, deletes it.

3. AI food recognition (third-party processing)

When you use photo or text recognition, the app sends the relevant food photo or text description directly from your device to the AI provider you selected — ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), or DeepSeek — authenticated with the API key you entered. This request does not pass through any server of ours. The selected provider processes the content under its own privacy policy:

  • OpenAI — openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Anthropic — anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • DeepSeek — deepseek.com

The app does not retain a copy of the photo on any server; the photo remains on your device unless you choose to send it for recognition.

4. Your API key

Your AI provider API key is stored only on your device and is sent only to that provider to authenticate your own requests. It is never sent to us and never shared with anyone else.

5. Device permissions

  • Camera — to photograph meals for AI recognition.
  • Photo library — to pick an existing meal photo.
  • Notifications — to remind you when a meal has not been logged. You can disable reminders in Settings.

Permissions are used only for these purposes and only when you trigger the related feature.

6. What we do not do

We do not collect analytics, we do not use advertising or tracking SDKs, we do not require an account, and we do not sell or share your data.

7. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Health disclaimer

Calorie and nutrition values are AI estimates for general reference only and are not medical advice. Consult a qualified professional for dietary or medical decisions.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version. Material changes will be noted in the app or this page.

10. Contact

Questions? Email regtechcrypto@gmail.com.

概要

食刻 NutriSnap 是一款个人饮食与营养记录工具。我们不运营任何服务器， 不收集、不存储、不出售你的个人数据。你记录的所有内容都保存在你自己的 设备上。AI 食物识别通过你自己的第三方 AI 账户完成（由你提供 API 密钥）。

1. 我们是谁

食刻 NutriSnap（"本应用"、"我们"）是一款独立的 iOS 与 Android 移动应用。 联系方式：regtechcrypto@gmail.com。

2. 保存在你设备上的数据

本应用仅在你的设备本地保存以下信息，绝不上传给我们：

  • 你的饮食日记（食物名称、重量、热量、蛋白质、脂肪、碳水、餐次、日期） 以及你为记录添加的照片；
  • 你的设置（每日目标、提醒时间、语言）；
  • 你选择的 AI 服务商及其 API 密钥。

我们无法访问这些数据。删除某条记录或卸载应用即会删除它。

3. AI 食物识别（第三方处理）

当你使用拍照或文字识别时，本应用会把相应的食物照片或文字描述 从你的设备直接发送给你所选择的 AI 服务商——ChatGPT（OpenAI）、 Claude（Anthropic）或 DeepSeek——并用你填写的 API 密钥进行身份验证。 该请求不经过我们的任何服务器。所选服务商将依据其各自的隐私政策处理这些内容：

  • OpenAI — openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Anthropic — anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • DeepSeek — deepseek.com

本应用不会在任何服务器上保留照片副本；除非你主动发送照片进行识别，否则照片 始终保留在你的设备上。

4. 你的 API 密钥

你的 AI 服务商 API 密钥仅保存在你的设备上，且只会发送给该服务商以验证你 自己的请求。它绝不会发送给我们，也不会分享给任何第三方。

5. 设备权限

  • 相机——用于拍摄食物照片以进行 AI 识别。
  • 相册——用于从已有照片中选择食物图片。
  • 通知——用于在你忘记记录某餐时提醒你。可在设置中关闭提醒。

权限仅用于上述用途，且仅在你触发相关功能时使用。

6. 我们不做的事

我们不收集统计分析数据，不使用广告或追踪 SDK，不要求注册账户，也不出售或 分享你的数据。

7. 儿童

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集他们的数据。

8. 健康免责声明

热量与营养数值均为 AI 估算，仅供一般参考，不构成医疗建议。涉及饮食或医疗 决策请咨询专业人士。

9. 政策变更

我们可能更新本政策。上方"最后更新"日期代表当前版本。重大变更将在应用内或 本页面提示。
